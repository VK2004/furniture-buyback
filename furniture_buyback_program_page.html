Common questions

Which items are eligible?
We accept solid wood and metal furniture including dining tables, desks, chairs, shelving, and bedroom furniture. Upholstered sofas, mattresses, and heavily modified items are reviewed case by case.
Is pickup really free?
Yes — pickup is free when combined with a new delivery. Standalone pickups are available for a small fee depending on your location.
How long does the process take?
Get an online quote in under 5 minutes. Pickup is typically scheduled within 3–5 business days. Store credit is issued on the same day as pickup.
What if my item doesn't qualify?
We'll help you donate it to a local charity partner or arrange responsible recycling — at no charge to you.
Can I use store credit on sale items?
Yes. Store credit can be applied to any item in our showroom or online, including sale and clearance items. It never expires.
